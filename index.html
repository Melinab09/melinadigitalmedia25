~My Reflection Of Digital Media~

During the time I have spent in digital media I have learned many new skills especially on programs like photoshop, illustartor, and dreamweaver. These are skills that are not only useful in digital media but are something I will be able to utilize throughout the rest of my life. Digital media has been a free space for me to be creative and attempt new skills. I like how it is different from other classes I take when there is always a right and wrong answer because in digital media there is no right and wrong. I have truly enjoyed all my time in the class and will continue to try new things and bulid up skills until the end of the year.
